EZ-Report

Hello, We are the EZ-Report coding team. Our mission for this websiteis to make it easier to report small issues like potholes, unpaved strees, crossing sign malfunctions, and stop light errors so they don't become larger issues. We want to make it so people in every city can easily and quickly report problems and move on with their days. Using this web page(hopefully it'll eventually be an app) is relatively simple. You just need to select the borough your issue is in and which city official(s) you would like to report it to. This website will send your email to the city official(s) within about 5-10 minutes.
