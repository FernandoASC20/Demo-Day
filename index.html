EZ-Report

This Is our Website called EZ-Report. This website is focused on making it easier for everyone to report any problems they might have with either potholes, dirty streets, or any other problems with general cleanlieness. Using this webpage(hopefull eventually app) is relativley simple. You just need to select the borough your issue is in and which city official(s) you would like to report it to. This website will send your email to the city official(s) within about 5-10 minutes.
